Subscription Site Redesign

Project Required /

My biggest project has been solely redesigning 11 subscription sites, transforming basic static pages into modern microsites. The redesign resulted in an 18% reduction in bounce rates on the subscription order forms, 15% increase in online order cash YoY (worth £24k) and a 25% improvement in the Visitor to Trial conversion rate against the previous year. 

This process included:

  • User research, including research into competitors
  • A/B testing
  • Wordpress child theme creation, using Divi as parent theme
  • SASS for child theme files
  • GIT process
  • Custom plugins for forms and pricing tables
  • Providing training on Wordpress and Divi theme
  • GA event tracking
  • Use of HotJar to monitor user experience
  • Implementation of basic currency detector

The redesign won Incisive Media's Marketing Initiative award in 2016.

“We have made a massive step in the right direction in the look and feel, responsiveness and professionalism of our subscriptions marketing, with the launch of the subscriptions microsites.” Group Marketing & Subscriptions Director
